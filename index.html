Admissions

Join our institution for quality technical education and a bright future. Applications are now open for the academic year 2023-24.

Admission Process

1

Entrance Examination

Candidates must qualify the Jharkhand Polytechnic Entrance Test (JPET) conducted by the Jharkhand Combined Entrance Competitive Examination Board (JCECEB).

2

Counseling

Based on the rank obtained in JPET, candidates are called for counseling where they can choose their preferred branch as per the availability.

3

Document Verification

Selected candidates need to submit all required documents for verification including academic certificates, domicile certificate, and category certificates if applicable.

4

Fee Payment

After successful verification, candidates need to pay the admission fee to confirm their seat in the allotted branch.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Candidate must have passed 10th/Matriculation with minimum 35% marks (relaxation as per government norms for reserved categories).
  • Mathematics and Science are compulsory subjects in 10th class.
  • Age limit: Minimum 15 years as on 1st July of the admission year.
  • Candidate must be a resident of Jharkhand (For state quota seats).
